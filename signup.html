How to Sign Up:

If you have any questions, feel free to email us.

  1. Choose a tree. It's important that your tree is convenient for you to visit. If you can see it from your home, or if it's along your daily route, that is perfect!
  2. Sign into OpenTreeMap.org (OTM) and map your tree. Fill out your tree’s profile as completely as possible, making sure to include a photo, the diameter of the trunk, and the species.
  3. Choose a name for your tree. This can be a human first name, such as Arlene or Demetrius. Email it to treespeech@sustainablejc.org, along with the unique URL for your tree on OTM
    (example: https://www.opentreemap.org/jctrees/features/1234567)
  4. We will email you back with a username for your twitter account. Please create the twitter account.
  5. Link everything up: In your twitter bio, type “#TreeSpeech” and “treespeech.net” as well as the OTM URL for your tree. Type your Twitter username into the “comments” field of your tree's OTM page.
  6. Start Tweeting!
  7. Use the hashtag #TreeSpeech in every tweet. This unifies the project and gives your tweets more visibility.
  8. Tweet in the first person, as if the tree were talking.
  9. Follow and say hi to other trees.
  10. We will create a laminated tag for you. We’ll hand it off to you in person or via the mail.
  11. Suggested donation is $15 per tree, to cover project costs. This is not required to participate, but it is appreciated!
